Why start a sustainable business?

Women are underrepresented in business; despite outnumbering men by around 1 million in the UK, there are half as many female entrepreneurs. Our mission is to increase representation in the sustainable business sector by providing information, workshops and networking events.

Starting a business can be daunting for new entrepreneurs and sustainability can be considered a complicating factor, or perhaps something that can be considered at later stages. However, there are several benefits of incorporating sustainability from the earliest stages...

  1. Saving the planet
  2. Financial Savings
  3. A loyal customer base
  4. Advantage over competitors
